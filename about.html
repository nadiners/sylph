Based in London, Sylph Editions publish fiction, monographs, theoretical essays, limited-edition art and photography books, and different forms of experimental writing. The publications are presented either as individual books or as an ongoing series. The emphasis is on works in which image and text coexist, conceived as one. Every work is meticulously produced, care given equally to content and to form. Sylph Editions is also a design studio that specializes in book design and production. SYLPH EDITIONS is an independent art and design studio that handles all forms of print and web(?) design with a special emphasis on books. Obviously, all of the books we publish are designed and produced by us. Based in London, we work both locally and internationally on a variety of projects. Our publications are available online, through this website, and at selected retailers listed on the bookshops page.
